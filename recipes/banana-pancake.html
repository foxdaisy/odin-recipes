Banana Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe is by far my favorite. It's simple, easy-to-make and thus perfect for busy (or lazy) mornings. This 4-ingredient recipe makes two thin pancakes and serves one person.

It can be scaled up though, so don't worry if you have to serve more people. Just multiply the ingredients and follow the instructions.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons oatmeal flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly buttered pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour half the batter onto the pan and cook until browned on both sides.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the remaining batter.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings.
